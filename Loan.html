Types of Agriculture Loan

  • Crop Loan: Short-term loan for cultivation expenses.
  • Agricultural Term Loan: Long-term loan for non-seasonal expenses.
  • Solar Pump Set Loan: For small irrigation projects.

  • NABARD: Offers schemes like Capital Investment Subsidy Scheme and Agriclinic and Agribusiness Centres Scheme.
